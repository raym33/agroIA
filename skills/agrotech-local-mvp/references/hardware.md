# Hardware

Choose hardware by protocol, not vendor cloud.

Preferred interfaces:

- `SDI-12`
- `RS485 / Modbus RTU`
- `LoRaWAN`
- `pulse`
- `4-20 mA`
- `0-10 V`

Typical classes:

- soil probes
- weather stations
- flow meters
- bridges for installed hardware

Typical recommendations:

- METER TEROS 12
- Milesight EM500-SMTC
- Dragino LSE01
- Campbell ClimaVue 50 G2
- Milesight WTS506
- Valley 3000 Flowmeter
- Badger Meter ModMAG M2000
- Dragino SW3L
- Milesight UC300 / UC100

Do not optimize for mandatory proprietary clouds.
